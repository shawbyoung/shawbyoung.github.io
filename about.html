Shaw Young is a designer from the DC metropolitan area with experience in printmaking, acrylic painting, figure drawing, sewing, Photoshop, Inkscape, AutoDesk Sketchbook, javascript, html, & css. His work explores current trends in fashion, social issues, identity, and post-postmodernism. He has been awarded the Scholastic Gold Key, National Scholastic silver medal, as well as has been selected for the Student Art Spotlight at the Moss Art Center. He graduated from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology and currently attends Virginia Polytechnic Institute studying Information Technology. He is the vice president of the Art Club @ VT.
